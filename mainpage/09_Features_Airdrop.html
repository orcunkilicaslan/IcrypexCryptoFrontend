What is Icrypex Airdrop?

It is a saving system that you can earn as much as your crypto assets in your Icrypex account with advantageous Airdrop rates!

With which cryptocurrency can I get an Airdrop?

You can earn airdrop in every cryptocurrency in your assets except MPAY.

When will my Airdrops deposit to my account?

At the end of the day your Airdrop deposit to your account.

When can I use the Airdrop?

You can use the airdrops deposited in your account at any time. But keep in mind that you will continue to earn as long as you keep your crypto assets on your account!

Is it enough to have cryptocurrency in my account to get Airdrop?

It is enough to have your cryptocurrency in your account, but make sure that it is not on your orders list as an open order. Your airdrop is calculated on your cryptocurrencies that do not have open limit orders.
